The Old Stone Bridge

Category: Location • Tier: Low • Mood: Unsettling

Overview

A crumbling single span bridge arches over a narrow river gorge. Locals swear they hear footsteps crossing at night, but the bridge always lies empty when they look.

At the table

Use this as a travel encounter that can become a mini dungeon or a social mystery, depending on who is haunting the bridge. The bridge works in any rural or frontier region.

  • Hook: travelers vanish when crossing after sundown.
  • Complication: the bridge collapses mid cross if angered.
  • Reward: hidden stash embedded in one of the support stones.

Variants

Swap the river for a chasm of fog, an acid stream, or a ravine filled with bones from an ancient battle. Change the haunting presence to match your campaign tone.

Quick stats
  • Works for: fantasy, dark fantasy
  • Session role: travel scene, micro dungeon
  • Prep time: 5 minutes
DM notes

Decide before play if the haunting is sympathetic, malicious, or a misdirect for something worse under the water.
